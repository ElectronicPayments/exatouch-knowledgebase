Create a New Customer

Keeping track of customers in Exatouch will help you monitor your customers' purchase histories and preferences. Follow these step to create a new customer:

  1. From Home, click or tap Customers.
  2. Select Customer Details.
  3. Fill in customer details. Required fields are indicated in blue.
  4. Customer details can also be captured by clicking the swipe icon , and then swiping the customer's driver's license or ID card.

    descriptionsNote: Swiping a customer's ID card will capture First Name, Last Name, Address, Date of Birth, and Driver's License. Zip code and other fields will not populate. Acceptable ID cards may include driver's licenses or state-issued ID cards which have a magnetic stripe at the back.

  5. Click Save.

lightbulb_iconTip: Building a database of customer information will enable you to use additional features in Exatouch, such as the customer loyalty program.
