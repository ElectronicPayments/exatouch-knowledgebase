1. Click/tap the Lock.

3. Enter PIN or Swipe your Employee Card.

Click/Tap the name of the staff member you wish you deactivate.

Confirm deletion by clicking Yes.

Note: Staff members are not permanently deleted.  Rather, they are deactivated so you can reference their history and re-activate them if needed.
