How to Manage the Time Clock

Staff record their paid time by How to Clock In and How to Clock Out. They track their paid breaks by Clock Out for a Break and Clock In from a Break . These time entries are recorded by the Time Clock. Time Clock records can be viewed and managed as follows:

1. From Home, click or tap Staff.

2. Select Time Clock.

3. Select the Date by clicking the drop-down menu.

4. Click to highlight the name of a staff member.

5. View Time Clock Records section for a list of all time entries recorded.

6. Manage Time Clock entries by selecting from the following options:

Delete Record: delete a time entry.

Print Shift: print a shift report.

Insert Record: create and insert a new time entry.

Adjust a Time Clock entry by adjusting Declared Tips, Time In, and Time Out. For more details see How to Adjust a Time Clock Entry.

Tip: You can restrict staff from accessing and modifying Time Clock Records by applying Manager-Only access to Settings.
See How to Restrict Access to System Settings for more information.
