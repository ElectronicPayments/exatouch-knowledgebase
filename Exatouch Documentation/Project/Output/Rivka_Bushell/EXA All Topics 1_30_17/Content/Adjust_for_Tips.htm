Adjust for Tips

When you check out for the day, Exatouch will prompt you to declare your cash tips for all credit card transactions that have been completed during your shift. Declaring your cash tips is optional.

Tips may be added to a receipt when completing authorizations.

To complete authorization and adjust for your tips:

  1. From the Register screen, click or tap the Register tab.
  2. Select Complete auth.

  3. Highlight the Order # by clicking on the desired order. There may be several orders listed.
  4. Click or tap Complete.
  5. The Tip screen will pop-up. Enter the Tip amount, then select OK.
