How to Adjust a Time Clock Entry

The Time Clock captures an employees paid working hours. Time is tracked when employees clock in and clock out. Sometimes Employees forget to clock or out, or clock in only after they start working their shift. In these cases Time Clock entries can be adjusted.

To adjust a Time Clock entry:

1. From Home, click or tap Staff.

2. Select Time Clock.

3. Select Date by clicking the drop-down menu and highlighting the desired date.

4. Highlight the name of the staff member by clicking or tapping on their record.

5. View the staffer's list of Time Clock Records for the selected day.

6. Click on the record you want to edit.

7. Adjust the Time Clock record by editing the following fields:

Time In: and Time Out:

Adjust the date by clicking the drop-down menu. Select the revised date on the Calendar pop-up window.

Adjust the times by clicking the drop-down menus. Select the revised time on the Clock pop-up window.

Declared Tips: Enter a new tip amount in the Declared Tips field.

8. Click Save Changes.

 

Also see How to Manage the Time Clock

Tip: You can restrict staff from adjusting Time Clock entries by setting Manager-Only access to Settings. See How to Restrict Access to System Settings for more information.
