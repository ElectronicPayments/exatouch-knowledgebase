How to Clock Out

Follow these instructions to Clock Out after your shift:

1. Click or tap the Lock icon.

2. Select Clock Out.

3. Enter PIN or Swipe your Employee Card.

4. If you have not declared your tips, a pop-up box will appear. Enter an amount in the Declare Tips field, and click/tap OK.

5. After confirmation of “Successful Check Out,” choose whether you want to print your Shift Report.  Your system needs to be connected to a printer in order to print a Shift Report.
