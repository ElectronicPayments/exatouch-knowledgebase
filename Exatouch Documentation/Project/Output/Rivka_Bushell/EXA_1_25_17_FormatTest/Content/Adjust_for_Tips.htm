When you check out for the day, Exatouch will prompt you to declare your cash tips. Declaring your cash tips is optional.

Tips may be added to a receipt when completing authorizations.

You can complete authorizations provided they are completed before batching out.

To complete authorization and adjust for your tips:

  1. From Register screen, click or tap the Register tab.
