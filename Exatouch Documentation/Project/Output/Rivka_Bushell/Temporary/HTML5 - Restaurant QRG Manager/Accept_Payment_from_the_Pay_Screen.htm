Accept Payment from the Pay screen

Payments can be accepted directly from the Register screen or from the Pay screen. 

To Accept Payment From the Pay Screen

1. From the Register Screen, the Pay tab.

2. Choose from options on thePay screen.

Payment Options

Cash: Enter cash amount on the Pay screen.

Credit: Swipe credit card or manually enter card info.  Receipt will print, and signature will be required.

Debit: Swipe debit card and enter PIN. Receipt will print.

EBT: Swipe EBT card and enter PIN in applicable.  Receipt will print.

eGift: Swipe gift card or manually enter card number.

Check: Enter Check reference/number.

Rewards: If your customer is a registered Reward s User, you may apply the available reward to the amount due. To apply a customer reward, the customer needs to be assigned to the order.

Payout: Payouts are issued at the discretion of your manager. Please check with your manager about using this option.

Discounts: Discount amounts will be subtracted from the amount due.

Office CC: This option will allow you to enter a credit card payment through . You will need an Authorization Code for this.

Gift Certificate: Enter the Gift Certificate Number. The available gift amount will be applied to the order, and any remaining balance will be displayed.

More Options:

Check Balance:  Check the balances on eGift cards and Gift Certificates.
