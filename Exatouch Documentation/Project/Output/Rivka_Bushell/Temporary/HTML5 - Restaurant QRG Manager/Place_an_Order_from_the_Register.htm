Place an Order from the Register

Orders will vary based on the menu options you've set up in . The following steps will help you place an order from your Register. Details of your order may vary, but the steps to placing an order will be similar:

1. Choose Order Type from: Dine In, Take Out, Pick up, or Delivery.

2. Select Menu Category. You may choose from Favorites, or other categories such as Appetizers, Sides or Salads.

3. Select one or multiple items from the menu.>

4. You may be prompted for additional order details as you select your items.  For example, if your customer orders a hamburger, additional “Groups” of items may appear, and then you can select “Medium Rare” and “Side of Pickles”.

5. To add another order of the same item, select Repeat line.

6. Remove an item by selecting Remove line.

7. Apply a discount by selecting an item and clicking Discount.

8. Select Save order to send the order to the kitchen.
