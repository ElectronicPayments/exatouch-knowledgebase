How to Add Inventory Costs and Quantities to an Item

1. From Home, Items.

2. Select Items List .

3. Choose an item by tapping on the item name. You may have to scroll through the Items List with your finger to locate your selection.

4. Click the Inventory tab.

5. Confirm that the item is assigned to a vender in the Vendor(s) section. If not, assign a Vendor to an Item.

6. In the Enter Cost section select the relevant cost option.

7. Click Save.

Cost Section

Recently Paid, Average Paid, and Least Paid: The prices in these fields are automatically calculated and entered by when the item is received from a Purchase Order.These fields can be selected, but not edited manually.

User Entered field can be selected, and can also be edited.

Inventory Quantities Section

Add quantity in the Add Some field; subtract quantity in the Delete Some field.

Notable fields:

Low Level: Filling in a Low Level amount will set up an alert to notify you when the item reaches a low level.

Reorder Quantity: This field will automatically populate when a reorder quantity is added to a Purchase Order. The Reorder Quantity field cannot be manually edited in this location.

See: Create a Purchase Order

Note: An item must be designated as an Inventory Item to have a cost and inventory quantity associated with it. If an item is designated as a non-invenotry item, the Cost and Inventory Quantity fields are disabled.
