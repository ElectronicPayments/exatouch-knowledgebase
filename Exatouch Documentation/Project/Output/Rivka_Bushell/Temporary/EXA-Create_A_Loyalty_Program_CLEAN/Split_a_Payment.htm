Split a Payment 

Customers may want to split the payment of their order. Orders are split from the Pay screen.

These instructions will show you how to split the payment of a check equally between several parties (2 or more people):

1.  From Home, Register. Make sure the order appears on the register.

Tip: If the order is not listed on the register, select the Get order tab to find a List of Open Orders.

2. Select the Pay tab.

3. Split by.

4. Specify the number of parties who want to split the check by choosing a number in the Split by box. The order will now be split equally between the designated parties.

5. Select payment method(s).

 

If all parties want to pay with Cash:

Click Cash. You'll see the order split up into Cash payments

Click Pay, and follow the system prompts to complete the first transaction.

Once the first transaction is completed, follow the system prompts to complete the next transaction. You may have to click Pay again.

Repeat as needed for each payment transaction.

 

If all parties want to pay with Credit:

Click Credit. You'll see the order split up into Credit Card payments.

Follow the system prompts to complete the first transaction.  Then follow the system prompts to complete the next transaction(s).

 

If one customer wants to pay with Cash, and another party wants to pay with Credit:

Click Cash. You'll see the order split up into Cash payments.

Next to the entry you want to change to Credit, click Delete.

Click Credit to create a Credit Card payment. The credit card payment will be listed.

Repeat as needed to designate transactions as either Cash or Credit.

When customers are ready to pay, click Pay.

Follow the system prompts to complete the first transaction.  Then follow the system prompts to complete the next transaction(s).
