How to Generate SKU Numbers locally

It may be useful to assign a local SKU number or Alt SKU number to an item.

For example, you may want to create your own SKU number if your item does not have a manufacturer's (mfg) SKU number assigned.

From the New Item screen, there are two ways to generate SKU numbers locally:

 

1. Manually

Type a custom SKU number in the SKU or Alt SKU fields.

 

2. Automatically

Click next to the SKU or Alt SKU field.
