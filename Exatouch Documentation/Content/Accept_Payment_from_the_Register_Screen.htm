Accept Payment from the Register Screen

When it’s time to accept payment from the customer, you can accept payment directly from the Register screen, or you can accept payment from the Pay screen

To accept payment from the Register:

  1. Select from Cash, Credit or Debit.
  2. Follow prompts to complete the payment.
  3. Receipt will print

Payment options on the Register Screen:

Cash: Enter cash amount and OK.

Credit: Swipe Credit Card and Print Receipt.  Signature will be required.

Debit : PIN will be required.
