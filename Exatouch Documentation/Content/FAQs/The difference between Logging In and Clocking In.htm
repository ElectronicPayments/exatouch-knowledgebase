The difference between Logging In and Clocking In

You will be prompted to Log Into when you turn on your system, or start it up for the day.

You may also be prompted to log into Exatouch when you access your Settings.

Logging in is not used for timekeeping purposes.

 

Clocking In is used timekeeping purposes. For example, an employee would Clock In to begin their paid shift, and would Clock Out to end their shift. Your or an employee may want to clock in for a break, or clock out for a break.
