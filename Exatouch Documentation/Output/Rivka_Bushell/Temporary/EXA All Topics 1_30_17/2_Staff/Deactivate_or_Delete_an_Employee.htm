Deactivate or Delete an Employee

Sometimes employees leave and are fired, and you need to delete or deactivate them from your system:

  1. From the Home screen, Staff.
  2. Select Staff List.
  3. Click on the name of the employee.
  4. Select the Menu option.
  5. Press Delete.
  6. Click Yes to confirm.

description Note: Staff members are not permanently deleted.  Rather, they are deactivated so you can reference their history and re-activate them if needed.
