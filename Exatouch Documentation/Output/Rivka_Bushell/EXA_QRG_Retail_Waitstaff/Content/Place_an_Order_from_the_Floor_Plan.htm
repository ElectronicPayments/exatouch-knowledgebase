Place an Order from the Floor Plan

You can also place an order from the Floor Plan. This method is helpful if you are waiting tables.

To place an order while waiting tables:

  1. Click or tap the table you are waiting on. If you cannot see your tables, you may have to navigate to theRegister screen, and select Floor Plan.
  2. Once you've selected the table, choose Order from the pop-up menu.
  3. You may now place an order from the Register. Click the following for instructions: Place an Order from the Register
