Navigate to the Home Screen

The Home screen gives you easy access to Exatouch features.

Your location within Exatouch can be identified easily by viewing the description on the bottom left of your screen.

Your Home screen is labeled HOME.

Navigate to your Home screen by simply click/tapping the Home Icon one or multiple times.

Clicking the Home Icon will also bring you to the previous menu page. You may have to click the Home Icon several times to bring you back to your Home screen.
