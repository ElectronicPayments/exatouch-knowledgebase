An item is an offering that a merchant provides to their customer. Your business's list of items makes up your Menu/Inventory. As part of Exatouch's comprehensive service offering, we provide complimentary Menu/Inventory import service. When you first sign up for Exatouch, our in-house specialists will program your Menu and import your Inventory. If you choose not to use our Menu/Inventory import service, your Items List will be blank and you'll need to start adding items to your system. Even after your Menu/Inventory import is complete, you'll probably want to add additional items to your system.

  1. From the Home screen click or tap Items.
  2. Select Item Details.
  3. Fill in the item details.
  4. When you've completed entering item details, click or tap Save.
Notable Fields

These fields capture important information about your item.

  • SKU: SKU numbers are barcodes. Barcodes can be scanned into the system using your barcode scanner, or barcodes can be entered by manually typing the the code into the field. SKU numbers are usually assigned by the manufacturer, but you can or generate your own custom SKU number by clicking .

    See How to Generate SKU Numbers locally

  • Alt SKU: Applying an Alt SKU in assigning an alternate barcode to an item. For example, you may want to apply an additional SKU to a holiday edition of an item.
  • Short Description: This is the name of the item as it appears on your register's tab and on your customer's receipt. Short description also appears on your Items List and in your Exatouch reports. The contents of this field are visible to your client.
  • Description: You can enter a longer description of your item in this field. The contents of this field are for internal use only; they will not be seen by your clients.
  • Category and SubCategory fields help you organize and keep track of your items.

    Categories and subcategories are created on the Categories screen:

    • Click Home Items Categories.

    See How to Create Categories and Subcategories.

    description Note: If you do not select a Category, your item will be assigned the Category: None.

  • Retail Price: Enter your retail price here. If this field is left blank, your item will be assigned the retail price of $0.
 Optional Fields

These fields are optional, but using these fields will help you track your items and inventory. You'll also be able to set up restrictions and tax rules for your items.

  • Manufacturer: Click to add a new manufacturer, or you can add a new manufacturer by clicking: Home > Settings > System > Custom Labels > Manufacturer

    See How to Add a Manufacturer

  • Sell options allows you set a condition for the sale of your item.
  • For example, if the sell option is Stop selling at 0, Exatouch will not allow the sale of the item when your inventory for that item is 0.

    There are several Sell options available:

    • Not an inventory item is the default setting in Exatouch. Not an inventory item is used if you are not tracking inventory for the item. For example, drop-ship items which are special ordered and not kept in stock are not an inventory item. Most restaurants do not consider their menu selections inventory items.
    • Stop selling at 0 will limit the sale of the item once inventory reaches 0.
    • Go negative will allow the sale of the item if the inventory is 0. This option is useful for those times when your stock exceeds your inventory total. You can always modify your inventory to reflect additional stock.

      See Modify Inventory Level

      Each item can be assigned a Sell Option. Sell options are modified in the Preferences section.

      See Modify Sell Options

    • Color and Size: These fields become important for Matrix Inventory Items, such as clothing.

      See:How to Define Matrix Inventory Items

    • Modifier Limit: Allows you to limit the number of modifiers associated with the item.

      See:

    • Tax: The application of tax is defined through selection of a Tax Group.

        You can select multiple Tax Group check boxes (i.e. Group A and Group B are both checked), so that multiple tax rates apply to a single item.

        Tax rates for each Group are defined in Settings:

        Home > Settings > Taxes

        See: How to Set Tax Rates

    • Sold by Fraction: Select this option if your item will be sold by weight. If this item is selected, you must set a Charge Rate for your item, such as $5.00 per lb.
    • Visible on register: Most items are visible on the register by default. Some items may be ingredients or specials which you will not want to display on the Register. Unchecking Visible on Register will ensure that that an item will not clutter your register unneccessarily.
    • 86: This field indicates that an item is out of stock.
