Add a Vendor

Vendors are suppliers who provide you with goods. Keeping track of your vendors will help you effectively and accurately manage your inventory. In items are ordered vendors who supply them.

Vendors can be added from several locations:

Add a Vendor from the Purchasing Module
  1. From the Home screen, Purchasing.
  2. Select Vendor Details.
  3. Fill in vendor details.

    Note: To create a PO for a vendor, an email address and phone number are required. Be sure to fill in the Email and Phone fields if you intend to create POs for the vendor.

  4. Click Save.

Add a Vendor from Item Details:

You can add a vendor as you are editing or adding an item in the Item's Details.

  1. Navigate to Item Details. Home > Items > Item Details.
  2. Choose the Inventory tab.
  3. From the Vendor(s) section, click Add.
  4. Select New Vendors.
  5. Fill in vendor details

    Note: To create a PO for a vendor, an email address and phone number are required. Be sure to fill in the Email and Phone fields if you intend to create POs for the vendor.

  6. Click Save.

 

Note: Every item must be linked to a vendor. Once an item is linked to a vendor, a PO can be created for those items. and submitted to the vendor. Once the vendor's information is in your system and you've linked an item to that vendor, you can create a Purchase Order (PO) for that vendor.
