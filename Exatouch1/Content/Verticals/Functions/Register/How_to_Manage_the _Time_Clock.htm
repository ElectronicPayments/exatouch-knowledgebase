Staff record their paid time by Clocking In and Clocking Out. They track their paid breaks by Clocking Out for a Break and Clocking In from a Break . These time entries are recorded by the Time Clock. Time Clock records can be viewed and managed as follows:

  1. From Home, click or tap Staff.
  2. Select Time Clock.
  3. Select the Date by clicking the drop-down menu.
  4. Click to highlight the name of a staff member.
  5. View Time Clock Records section for a list of all time entries recorded.

Manage Time Clock entries by selecting from the following options:

  1. Delete Record: delete a time entry.
  2. Print Shift: print a shift report.
  3. Insert Record: create and insert a new time entry.
  4. Adjust a Time Clock entry by adjusting Declared Tips, Time In, and Time Out. For more details see How to Adjust a Time Clock Entry.

lightbulb_outline Tip! You can restrict staff from accessing and modifying Time Clock Records by applying Manager-Only access to Settings.

See How to Restrict Access to System Settings for more information.
