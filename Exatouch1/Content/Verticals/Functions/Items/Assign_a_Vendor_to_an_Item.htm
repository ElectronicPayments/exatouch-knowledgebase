Assign a Vendor to an Item

Assigning a vendor to an item gives you the ability to create a Purchase Order (PO) for that item.

1. From Home, Items.

2. Select Items List .

3. Choose an item by tapping on the item name. You may have to scroll through the Items List to locate your selection.

4. Click the Inventory tab.

5. Review the Vendor(s) listed.

Note: Because an item can be supplied by different vendors, multiple vendors may be listed for a single item.

6. If the desired vendor is listed, tapto highlight the vendor.

7. If the desired vendor is NOT listed, click Add to add a vendor to the Vendor(s) list.

8. The Select Vendors screen will pop-up. Click to highlight a vendor from the vendors list..

 

 

.

Tip: If your desired vendor is not listed, or if the vendor does not yet exist in your system, choose New Vendors to quickly add a new vendor. Also see: Add a Vendor

9. Once your vendor is highlighted, click Save.

Note: Notice that one of the vendors is marked (default). Each item in is associated with a default vendor. You can choose or reassign the default vendor. See: How to Set a Default Vendor for an Item
